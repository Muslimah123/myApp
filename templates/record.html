Instructions: Record your voice transaction

The recorded voice should be in this format "Bought item cost" or "Sold item cost" or "Capital cost" For example you can say Bought sugar 200 if you did a purchase or Sold sugar 20 if you did a sale of sugar
Note: Please adhere to this for your transaction to be successfully recorded by the system.
On the next page you have to click on a red button to start recording, The button will turn green after clicking on it to show that you are recording. After that click on stop button to stop recording.

After you are done recording the trascription of your voice will be displayed to you on the text box written Trascribed Text here.
After you are done click on process, the system will handle the rest of your transaction storage.
By clicking on proceed to record you are confirming that you will use the recording format specified in this manual
